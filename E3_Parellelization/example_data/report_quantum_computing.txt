RESEARCH REPORT: Quantum Computing Applications
Date: December 2024
Author: Dr. Michael Zhang, Quantum Computing Research Lab

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This report surveys current and projected applications of quantum computing across various industries, including drug discovery, financial modeling, and optimization problems.

KEY FINDINGS

1. Drug Discovery
- Quantum algorithms reduce simulation time by 40-60%
- Potential to accelerate development of new therapeutics
- Current limitations: error rates and scalability

2. Financial Services
- Quantum computing enables complex portfolio optimization
- Risk analysis capabilities exceed classical computers
- Implementation timeline: 5-10 years for practical applications

3. Optimization Problems
- Supply chain optimization improves efficiency by 15-30%
- Manufacturing process optimization yields cost savings
- Logistics planning significantly enhanced

CHALLENGES
- Quantum error correction remains a major obstacle
- High infrastructure and maintenance costs
- Limited availability of quantum developers
- Need for hybrid quantum-classical approaches

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Increase investment in quantum hardware development
2. Develop quantum-ready algorithms
3. Build workforce training programs
4. Establish industry-academia partnerships
5. Create regulatory frameworks for quantum technologies

CONCLUSION
Quantum computing promises transformative capabilities but faces significant technical and practical challenges that require sustained research and investment.
